## 4. Census-anomaly drop-gate — proven to have TEETH before trusting anomaly=0

Because source-trace traces only symmetry-DISAGREEMENT edges (blind on the 20 passing cities),
the corpus-wide drop check for those cities is the touch-census **`anomaly`** column (a road
point-crossing whose clipped fragment is in NEITHER flanking cell = the drop signature).

Per the PI gate, `anomaly=0` must mean "looked and found none," not "cannot detect." Commit
`8573ec0` extracts the census classification into a pure `classify_point_crossings()` (the exact
function `touch_census` calls — byte-identical census output) and pins it with synthetic-orphan
teeth tests (`tests/data/multiregion/test_touch_census_teeth.py`, 7 tests):
orphan→anomaly, three-regime distinguishment, axis-1 flanking, nonroad/interval exclusion
direction, clean-corpus→0. **Red-before:** misrouting the `else: anomaly` branch to
`touch_as_cross` reds 4/7 tests (anomaly reports 0 where a real orphan exists); reverting
restores green. The gate is non-vacuous.
